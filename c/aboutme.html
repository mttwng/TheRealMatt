Anand Lakshminarayan
UC Berkeley Electrical Engineering Computer Science Junior Student

I started this venture when my friend saw that I was getting a lot of interviews and asked me to look over his resume. After I worked with him, he immediately got several interviews, one of which led to his first internship.

Since then, I have expanded my business through friend referrals and Facebook posts to try and help as many people as I can. I found that though the majors and schools may differ, the underlying problems amongst my clients' resumes were strikingly similar. After getting numerous positive responses and establishing myself, I made the switch over to a paid service.

My goal is to help people get as many interviews as possible to better leverage their skills in today's job market. If you like what you see, I encourage you to sign up and send over your resume. You'll be wondering why you didn't work with me earlier.
